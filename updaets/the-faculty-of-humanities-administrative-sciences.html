Faculty of Administrative and Human Sciences Overview
The Faculty of Administrative and Human Sciences at the University of Science and Technology - Aden - seeks to provide a distinguished educational service that gives students knowledge and skills in administrative and human sciences, in a manner that meets the needs of the community and the local, regional and international labor market, and contributes to the development of society’s curricula and providing an adequate education and resource provider. In accordance with local, regional and international standards, in accordance with comprehensive quality standards.
Vision
To be a pioneer in the administrative and accounting fields, humanities and research activities locally, regionally and globally.
Valuse
  • Teamwork.
  • Honesty and transparency.
  • professional.
  • Excellence.
  • The responsibility.
  • Creativity.
Our Goals
  • Providing a distinguished educational service that provides students with knowledge and skills in administrative and human sciences.
  • Contribute to supporting scientific research efforts in the field of administrative and human sciences.
  • Contribute to strengthening the role of the university in community service by providing advisory and training programs in the field of administrative sciences.
A word from the Dean of the College of Administrative Sciences and Humanities
The College of Administrative and Human Sciences seeks to provide a distinguished educational service that leads to the provision of competencies to the labor market and in a manner that contributes to raising the development capabilities of the country and society, and working for excellence in the educational process at the local, regional and international levels, and the display in the educational process of ethical, technical and professional behaviors and dealing responsibly towards the process Educational, excellence in quality, initiative and teamwork between students, professors, and university administration.

The College of Administrative Sciences and Humanities is interested in two aspects of science, the first of which is administrative sciences, which includes various programs in administrative sciences, including business administration, both Arabic and English, accounting, international business administration, marketing, production management, and other administrative sciences.
Administrative sciences are considered an essential basis for the activity of any institution, private or public business organization, or any government agency, because the requirements for conducting business in any activity are the presence of administrators, accountants, economists...etc. The development and growth of any country, organization or work body comes through the efficiency and capabilities of its administrative and accounting staff, the extent of their dedication to their work, their sincerity, sincerity and honesty.
The second aspect is the humanities, under which different disciplines fall under such as linguistics, legislation, law, Islamic studies and other sciences related to human sciences.

The humanities provide the qualifications needed by business organizations and state agencies, such as legislators, legal experts, linguists, and others.
Admission and registration requirements
A student wishing to join the faculty is required to meet the following conditions:
  • The faculty accepts high school holders in the Republic of Yemen or its equivalent at the rate that is determined annually.
  • Submit all the required documents, certified with the application for admission to the faculty, to the Admission and Registration Department.
  • Admission is made according to the capacity specified by the university and approved by the Ministry of Higher Education.
  • The student undertakes to abide by the regulations, instructions and regulations of the university in force during the study.

  • International accreditation and status of Faculty of Humanities and Administrative Sciences
    Within a few years of its inception, the Faculty of Humanities and Administrative Sciences at the University of Science and Technology - Yemen has been successful and competitive in offering business and liberal sciences programs in various specializations in Yemen, using both Arabic and English mediums of instruction. The faculty of humanities and administrative sciences has gained a robust reputation of academic excellence both locally in Yemen and internationally.Learners are equipped with skills in recognizing, evaluating and constructing arguments; reasoning; critical activity, judgement, logic and argument.

    Throughout its course of development, The faculty of humanities and administrative sciences-Yemen, has gone through a major restructuring exercise under the University's Strategic Plan which aimed at increasing the programmes offered. The departments of accounting, humanities and administrative sciences, represent the core domains of the faculty, and offer students the opportunity of taking a fresh look at the nature of the programmes and to explore the possibilities of making programmes both more exciting and marketable.

    As such, the Faculty of Humanities and Administrative Sciences-Yemen strives to offer prospective learners with accredited and up-to-date academic programmes leading to qualifications that are stimulating, and which will ultimately allow for individual intellectual growth, and at the end of the day, graduates of the fields of business administration, and social sciences are made marketable in their careers of choice, and responsible citizens in their respective societies.

    To this end, The Faculty of Humanities and Administrative Sciences-Yemen sets you on your pathway to shaping the future of your community. Its broad-based curricula blend knowledge with practical experience and relevance, equipping you with the confidence and skills required of careers in education and the social sciences.
