About the College of Medicine and Health Sciences
The College of Medicine and Health Sciences at the University of Science and Technology seeks to prepare a medical and health cadre with distinguished competencies and capabilities, contributing to serving the community by providing an appropriate educational and research environment, a competent cadre, and modern curricula oriented towards the community, using the latest educational technologies and in accordance with quality and accreditation standards.
Vision
Regional leadership and global excellence in medical and health education.

College Objectives
  • Providing the community with health cadres with distinguished professional skills and competencies.
  • Providing distinguished academic health programs in accordance with quality standards and academic accreditation.
  • Contributing to providing and developing health services in the community.
  • Providing a learning and research environment that meets the needs of society.
  • Activating communication with local and academic institutions related to health care.
Dean's Word
Technology was established as the first private medical college in Yemen in 1994 AD with the establishment of the university. Its founders established it out of their awareness that the education system is the most important means of shaping the national identity, and out of their desire to contribute to achieving the desired national change at the level of education, health and development.

Since its inception, our college has been known for its uniqueness and precedence in attracting academic programs, adopting modern teaching and learning strategies, implementing quality and academic development systems, and the diversity of community service programs that have provided services to all segments of society (starting with free scholarships for top students and memorizers of the Holy Quran, to medical camps that provide free health services to thousands of beneficiaries), its local, regional and international partnerships, the diverse educational environment in terms of study and teachers, the infrastructure that distinguishes our college, etc. Read More

Advanced equipment and modern teaching methods in the College of Medicine and Health Sciences, Yemen
The College of Medicine and Health Sciences - Yemen is keen to provide a wide range of means to support the educational process, the most important of which are laboratories equipped with the latest means and technologies, quality education and scientific material, in addition to providing a stimulating educational environment to acquire knowledge and efficient training to work in the medical fields, aiming to graduate a competent cadre to meet the requirements of society and the labor market.

The College of Medicine and Health Sciences is also a model educational center, where equipment, laboratories and devices are available for each program. It also provides scientific material in its theoretical and scientific aspects through a set of means, the most important of which are lectures, seminars, scientific visits, scientific training, laboratories and specialization periods.

To ensure the quality of scientific outputs, the college provides a set of the most advanced means in the field of medicine and health sciences, including modern, integrated laboratories equipped with computers, in addition to departments dedicated to anatomy, biochemistry, physiology, pharmacy, and medical laboratories.

The programs of the College of Medicine and Health Sciences in Yemen provide prospects for teaching and scientific research in a wide range of specializations; as the college programs are designed according to a methodology that aims to enable the graduate to employ practical skills to conduct research and medical work independently. The student's performance and academic achievement are also supervised on an ongoing basis and through a mechanism that enables him to practice the medical profession according to high professional and ethical standards.
Features of studying at the College of Medicine and Health Sciences
  • Students at the College of Medicine and Health Sciences enjoy a wide range of advantages, the most important of which are the availability of infrastructure, specialized professional staff, modern equipment and devices, in addition to the University of Science and Technology's private hospital, which is equipped with specialized professional staff for training.

    The College of Medicine and Health Sciences, affiliated with the University of Science and Technology, is distinguished as the first college of medicine in Yemen. This is attributed to a number of factors, the most important of which are: the stimulating educational environment equipped with the latest laboratories, the qualified and experienced teaching staff, and a group of other factors that have collectively led to the continuation of the development horizon and the adoption of modern patterns for studying medicine and health sciences that keep pace with modern teaching methods.
