About the Center
The strategic goal of the center is to work on transforming into a commercial publishing house specialized in writing and printing academic university books according to the specifications of academic accreditation quality standards, after approving the descriptions and scientific material of the books composed by the scientific departments in all specializations at the university, and the continuous development of these publications in a way that achieves their goal, and serves the educational process in our country in general, and in a way that achieves customer satisfaction in the scientific material, artistic production and prices with a qualified and distinguished administrative and technical staff.
Our Message
The University Book Center seeks to provide textbooks and their supporting media to local and regional universities and researchers in a way that meets the needs of the effective educational process in education by providing sources of knowledge in accordance with international academic and technical standards and foundations and achieving comprehensive quality, with a competent and advanced administrative staff.
Our Vision
To make the University of Science and Technology University Book Center one of the most important sources of knowledge locally and regionally.
Valuse
  • Credibility and Commitment
  • High Professionalism
  • Continuous Development
Center's Objectives
  • Production of educational books and CDs.
  • Printing educational books and CDs.
  • Marketing educational books and CDs.
  • Supporting the University of Science and Technology and other universities in providing books and accompanying educational media in the appropriate place and time according to the requirements of higher education at the main center in Aden and branches inside and outside Yemen.


Sub-Objectives
  • Improving the university's service to students by providing all learning resources in one location close to the university campus.
  • Facilitating the mission of the university's branches inside the country and its external offices in obtaining knowledge sources.
  • Activating the marketing of the university's products from knowledge sources to members of other universities internally and externally.
  • Increase university revenues other than student fees.
Beneficiaries
  • Scientific departments at the University of Science and Technology and its branches
  • Other Yemeni universities that have concluded twinning agreements with the university
  • Students of the University of Science and Technology at the main center - Aden - and its branches inside and outside Yemen.
  • Researchers and academics in general
Center Services
  • Follow up with academics in writing books and university courses.
  • Printing university books and curricula after they have been approved by the relevant academic departments at the university.
  • Distributing books to university branches and other university you wish to visit.
  • Follow up on updating and developing university books and curricula in cooperation with academics working at the university.
