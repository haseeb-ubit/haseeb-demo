Faculty of Engineering and Computing Overview
The mission of the Faculty of Engineering and Computing at the Yemeni University of Science and Technology is to prepare distinguished specialists in the various fields of engineering, technology specializations, information systems and graphics, equipped with the foundations of knowledge and skills, and qualified to meet the requirements of the labor market, through a specialized staff, a distinguished educational environment and advanced programs, and in providing consultations and scientific research that Contribute to community service in accordance with comprehensive quality systems and professional and ethical requirements.
University Vision
The Faculty of Engineering and Computing at the University of Science and Technology is a pioneer locally and regionally distinguished.
Valuse
  • Quality and Excellence
  • Continuous education
  • creativity and innovation
  • Global Partnership
  • social responsibility
  • Teamwork
  • Transparency
  • Accountability and Equalization
Objectives
  • Providing students with knowledge and skills in scientific and engineering fields.
  • Preparing qualified engineers to meet the requirements of the labor market.
  • Providing the community with highly qualified specialists in the fields of engineering and computers who are able to deal with specialized problems.
  • Contribute to supporting scientific research efforts in the scientific and engineering fields.
Dean's Word
Praise be to God, Lord of the Worlds, and prayers and peace be upon the honest and trustworthy our master Muhammad and all his family and companions, and after:

At the outset, I welcome all visitors to the website of the College of Engineering and Computers at the University of Science and Technology.

The college provides modern and diverse programs in engineering fields, which include civil engineering, architecture, biomedical engineering, mechatronics engineering, as well as in the fields of computers, information technology, information systems, graphic design and multimedia to obtain a bachelor's degree. This is in addition to the master's program in computing.

The college invested thanks to the continuous support provided by the university leadership represented by the university president, to work on equipping laboratories and workshops, as well as advanced laboratories in all engineering and computer fields,
and classrooms laboratories in all engineering and computer fields, and classrooms equipped with modern technologies, activating educational strategies and focusing on educational curricula and cadres in order to keep pace with engineering and computer development and prepare them To be among the future makers, elite academics who are distinguished in their scientific fields contribute to this. The college is also keen to achieve high standards of quality and accreditation, as the programs under this college have been developed according to a scientific methodology that meets the needs of the local and regional labor market and in accordance with the standards approved by the academic accreditation organization, and the college is now preparing and preparing for the rest of the accreditation requirements according to a plan prepared in advance for this purpose.

The mission of the college is not limited to preparing students to enter the labor market, but extends beyond their graduation by providing them with the professional and cognitive skills necessary to meet all forms of challenges in engineering, industrial and computer sciences,
which qualifies them to lead in the fields of creativity and engineering and computer innovation committed to professional ethics.

If you are looking for a unique university study experience in one of the best engineering and computer colleges at the local and regional levels, I invite you to the college to explore the study opportunities we offer, or visit the college website to get the information you are looking for.

I ask God Almighty to help us to advance this ancient scientific edifice and for the college to be a beacon and a model to guide students and society for a better path towards a more beautiful future.

In conclusion, I thank you once again for your visit to the Faculty of Engineering and Computers website, and we hope you will repeat your visit to this website and continue your communication with the Faculty, its administration and faculty members.

Dean of the College
Assistant Dr. Nasr Al-Saqqaf
Admission Requirements
  • The faculty accepts high school holders in the Republic of Yemen or its equivalent at the rate that is determined annually.
  • Submit all the required documents, certified with the application for admission to the university, to the Admission and Registration Department.
  • Admission is made according to the capacity specified by the university and approved by the Ministry of Higher Education.
  • The student undertakes to abide by the regulations, instructions and regulations of the university in force during the study.
