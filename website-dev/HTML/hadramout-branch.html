About Hadramout Branch
In light of the increasing interest that the University of Science and Technology in Yemen pays to developing human resources in our local community in the field of higher education, which annually provides the community with qualified and specialized cadres, the University of Science and Technology - Hadhramaut Branch was opened in the academic year 2000/2001 AD, starting with the distance learning system in the city of Mukalla, which began with seven specializations: (Islamic Studies, Arabic Language, History, Media, Sharia and Law, Accounting and Business Administration)). Student Affairs - Hadramout Branch
Laboratories
Hadhramout University of Science and Technology has paid great attention to the practical aspect of academic education. It has given it the utmost attention by equipping a group of computer labs with the latest means and specifications to facilitate the acquisition of practical skills by its students with the comfort of learning and modern technology.

It provides high-spec computers from the latest versions of computers in the world of computers and provides them with a high-speed Internet network to meet the educational and training requirements in academic education and encourage the scientific research process among its students. It is distinguished by its ability and speed of performance in operating the specialized application and training programs provided with it to serve the academic specialization courses at the university.
Library
The libraries were established with the establishment of the university in 1994 AD and have since sought to be the first to possess everything new in the service of the educational and academic field and the continuous pursuit of providing sources and containers of information in all its types and forms. The university libraries include more than 90 thousand sources of information in various fields of science and knowledge. These sources vary between books, periodicals and CDs. The libraries also have a computerized program that serves all technical works from entering book data, research and lending. We are currently seeking in the library administration to establish an electronic library containing many global databases. From here, I invite all faculty members to visit the library regularly and urge students to benefit from its holdings and view what is new to develop their scientific and cultural knowledge. We hope that you will find everything you are looking for and rest assured that all your comments and inquiries will be welcomed and cared for.
Library Management System Interface in Hadramout Branch chevron_right
Tasks of the librarian in Hadhramaut branch:
  • The library is open daily for students, academics, employees and library visitors from outside the branch.
  • Arrange books and library every day.
  • Lending required books to students, faculty members and employees according to the library system in force.
  • Follow up on the return of borrowed books.
  • Monitoring the library door as visitors enter and exit the library.
  • Photographing books, periodicals and university theses for library patrons based on uploading by academic departments.
  • Request to subscribe to magazines and periodicals based on the submission of scientific departments.
  • Entering new books into the library system, classifying, numbering and stamping them.
  • Submitting the names of borrowers who are late in returning books.
  • Create signboards and library instructions.
  • Submitting proposals for library development.
  • Sorting torn books and recommending a suitable solution for them.
  • Proposing the purchase of books in coordination with department heads.
Admission and Registration Procedures - Hadramout Branch
Documents required for high school graduates
  • The original high school certificate or its equivalent, certified by the relevant authorities, along with a certified copy, and a translation and certification of the certificate if it is not in Arabic.
  • A copy of the national ID card, family card or passport.
  • Photos of the first eight pages of the passport (for non-Yemenis), provided that the passport is valid for more than one year and indicates residence in Yemen for at least one year.
  • 4 recent personal photos, size (4x6), with a white background
If the secondary school certificate is from outside Yemen, it must be certified (original + copy)
  • From the Ministry of Education in the country that issued the certificate.
  • From the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in the country that issued the certificate.
  • From the Embassy of the Republic of Yemen in the country that issued the certificate.
  • Yemeni Foreign Ministry.
  • Its equivalence in the Yemeni Ministry of Education.
Admission and registration requirements (Bachelor's):
  • The university accepts holders of a high school diploma or its equivalent according to the percentages specified by the Ministry of Higher Education.
  • The student undertakes to abide by the university's regulations, instructions and rules.
