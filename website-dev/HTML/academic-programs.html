International Programs Offered at University of Science and Technology - Yemen
The gateway to studying at internationally recognized universities starts here; as the University of Science and Technology is the first university in Yemen to offer a group of international programs in various fields, which provide students with future prospects to gain knowledge and reach an internationally accredited degree.
University Vision
The University of Science and Technology firmly believes in the importance of establishing joint links with local and international universities and educational organizations. Therefore, the University of Science and Technology establishes a number of links with educational institutions at the local and international levels. This cooperation has resulted in beneficial exchange of student and staff programs, joint research, graduate programs, and joint undergraduate programs.
What will the student achieve by applying to international programs?
  • International participation and affiliation
    Strong links with international institutions will give students and staff the opportunity to actively participate in international organizations and networking prospects of various kinds. Furthermore, this cooperation will allow for exchange programs for both students and staff, joint research and postgraduate programs, as well as international conference organizations.
  • Get high quality education
    In terms of quality of education, the project urges cooperating institutions to redistribute their resources to achieve more beneficial and effective tasks and goals for students by adhering to the standards of cooperation, which together seek to improve the quality of academic education in the fields of research and study programs.
  • Specialized and university accreditation
    Collaboration with an internationally leading university of science and technology will ensure that graduate programs adhere to the requirements set by the relevant accreditation bodies, and that their programs are subject to periodic reviews by specialists; all of which will enhance and document the international accreditation of the program and the university.
List of international universities implementing international cooperation programs - University of Science and Technology in Yemen
Over the years, the University of Science and Technology - Yemen has succeeded in providing the best educational opportunities and levels to its students through international cooperation programs, by forming cooperation links with several universities at the local and international levels.

Here is a list of the most important international universities that had a significant impact on active participation:
  • University of Science and Technology Malaysia USIM
  • Open University of Malaysia OUM
  • Sultan Zainal Abidin University UNISZA
  • University of Science Malaysia USM
  • National University of Malaysia UKM
