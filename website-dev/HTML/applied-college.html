About the Center
  • Graduating specialized professional cadres who possess the knowledge and skills that qualify them to compete for employment opportunities locally and regionally.
  • Self-building and promoting positive behaviors for community development.
  • Providing government institutions and business organizations with specialized professional staff.
  • Activating partnerships with similar institutions working in the field of vocational education and relevant local and international organizations.
Vision
The College of Applied Science and Technology aspires to be the leading college at the local and regional levels by offering technical and professional programs that meet the requirements of the labor market and keep pace with technological developments.
Message
The College of Applied Science and Technology seeks to contribute to meeting the needs of the local and regional market for qualified professional cadres; through an advanced educational and technical environment, a competent human cadre, and community service relations in accordance with quality standards and academic accreditation.
Dean's Word
In the name of God, the most gracious, the most merciful

It is with great joy, pleasure and happiness that I write these simple words to announce the opening of the official website of the College of Applied Sciences and Technology - a private community college - Aden, which includes comprehensive information about the college in terms of departments, academic programs, study plans, and the college's vision, mission and goals.

I am also honored to be the Dean of the College during this period to work within the College's staff and its wonderful team, which has been the first to translate the College's vision and mission until they reached this level of distinguished performance.

The College of Applied Science and Technology - Aden - strives, through the programs it offers, to meet the needs of the labor market and contribute to eliminating unemployment; this is done through:

- Choosing modern and advanced educational curricula that keep pace with the requirements and needs of the labor market in accordance with quality standards and academic accreditation.

- Creating an attractive educational environment in light of the current challenges, by providing a number of technical laboratories equipped with the latest advanced devices and equipment, necessary for training students on them.

- Attracting competent educational cadres with high academic qualifications, distinguished experience and specialization in vocational education in line with quality requirements and commitment to academic accreditation standards.

- Continuous development of the capabilities of faculty members and administrative staff to keep pace with rapid changes in the field of technology, as they are one of the main pillars and important inputs to the college.

- Contracting with many public and private hospitals to facilitate the application and training process for college students within these hospitals.

Finally, the College Deanship is fully prepared to receive comments, opinions and suggestions through this website, and we will consider them an important source for developing institutional and academic work within the College. God is the Grantor of success and the Guide to the right path.
