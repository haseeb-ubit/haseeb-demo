University of Science and Technology Aden: 25th place in the Arab world
In the Arab University Ranking for the year 2024, the University of Science and Technology - Aden ranked 25th in the Arab world, advancing 34 places in one year. It also achieved first place locally among private universities, and first among Yemeni universities in the participation index.

By admin / December 22, 2024

It is worth noting that the Arab University Ranking is published annually by the "Arab University Ranking Council ARU", which was established through joint cooperation between the Association of Arab Universities, the League of Arab States, the Arab League Educational, Cultural and Scientific Organization (ALECSO) and the Federation of Arab Scientific Research Councils; with the aim of improving the outcomes of higher education and scientific research, through this classification.

The classification is based on 4 main performance indicators in evaluating universities, which are: the education and learning index, the scientific research index, the creativity and innovation index, and the participation index.

It is worth noting that the number of universities included in the classification has increased this year compared to last year 2023, both at the Arab and local levels.

The University of Science and Technology - Aden has also recently achieved first place in the QS World University Rankings, and with this new ranking it confirms its worthiness and enhances its solid position on the Yemeni and Arab levels.
