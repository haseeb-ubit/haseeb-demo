Introduction
As a leading international institution, UTS has expanded its offering of international programmes not only at the national and regional level, but has also extended to form collaborative links with international universities in countries such as China, Malaysia, Turkey, the Netherlands, Germany and Italy. The university has offered a number of postgraduate and research programmes to Malaysian and other universities, with over 400 graduates from these programmes.

The International Programs Department is responsible for permanent coordination with universities and scientific institutions that offer scientific programs and specializations to be taught in the Deanship of Graduate Studies at the University of Science and Technology, and for developing or downloading study plans, scientific materials, and curricula that the department will implement for students studying in its academic specializations, and for harmonizing, approximating, and coordinating between the systems, regulations, times, and activities of the universities offering study programs and specializations and between the systems, regulations, times, and activities of the University of Science and Technology implementing those programs and specializations, and developing and formulating schedules of lectures, exams, and various activities for the specializations and programs affiliated with the department and managing and following up on their implementation.
Admission Requirements
  • PhD Admission Requirements
    • A copy of the first page of the passport with a validity of at least one and a half years.
    • White background selfie without glasses
    • A copy of the Bachelor's degree certificate in English and the original language.
    • A copy of the bachelor's degree transcript in English and the original language.
    • A copy of the master's degree certificate in English and the original language
    • A copy of the transcript of grades for the master's degree in English and the original language
    • English language certificate (TOEFL or IELTS test) (if available)
    • Certificate of practical experience (if available)
  • Undergraduate Admission Requirements
    • A copy of all passport pages with a validity of at least one and a half years.
    • White background selfie without glasses
    • A copy of the high school certificate and transcript in English and the original language.
    • English language certificate (TOEFL or IELTS test) (if available).
    • Application form
    • Registration payment receipt
  • Admission requirements for master's degree
    • A copy of the first page of the passport with a validity of at least one and a half years.
    • White background selfie without glasses
    • A copy of the Bachelor's degree certificate in English and the original language.
    • A copy of the bachelor's degree transcript in English and the original language.
    • English language certificate (TOEFL or IELTS test) (if available)
    • Certificate of practical experience (if available)
    • Thesis or proposal (for research system only)
    • A copy of the application fee payment receipt
Admission and registration for the academic year 2024/2025 is now open in all university faculties and specializations
