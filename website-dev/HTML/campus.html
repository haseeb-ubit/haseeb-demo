University of Science and Technology Yemen (UST) Campus Life
Introduction
The University of Science and Technology of Yemen aspires to become one of the most prestigious universities at the regional and global levels, and works to achieve this goal by raising the level of performance in all aspects, especially academic ones, by providing the appropriate environments to provide distinguished education and effective learning, implement advanced studies and research projects, and provide distinguished services to the community.

The campus includes everything a student needs for learning, training or activity, from classrooms with international educational specifications, a central library that serves all specializations, and a central library that serves the university's specializations, in addition to the graduate studies hall and the grand hall for training university students, a seminar hall for scientific activities and seminars. Engineering and electronics laboratories and computer labs. The university also provided sports equipment and a gym. Audio-visual halls. Research and development centers. A seminar hall, training halls and a workshop hall. Modern and advanced laboratories, spacious green spaces, a distinguished student lounge and many other features.

National and international conferences
