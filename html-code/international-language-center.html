About The Center
The International Language Center is one of the most important English language centers that constantly seeks to improve the quality of education. The university realizes how much some groups of students need language support and skill improvement when preparing to apply for any international scholarship in the English language. In addition, the university recognizes the important role played by teachers, counselors and parents in encouraging and supporting students. Therefore, at the International Language Center, we provide them with everything students need from guidance, counseling, awareness and other services to ensure quality education.
Our Message
Excellence in teaching English locally and regionally.
Our Vision
Providing English language training courses using advanced international curricula taught by highly experienced and qualified teachers who use modern educational methodologies that are compatible with international quality standards.
Our Goals
  • Providing the labor market with qualified and highly efficient graduates in the field of English language.
  • Preparing the student to compete in the labor market.
  • Contributing to meeting the needs of the community and the local and regional labor market for qualified personnel.
  • Expanding partnerships with similar educational institutions locally and regionally.
  • Expanding partnerships with other educational institutions locally and internationally.
