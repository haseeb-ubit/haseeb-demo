Our Vision
Leadership and excellence in graduate studies and scientific research.
Objectives
  • Meeting the needs of higher education institutions and the labor market with graduate degrees.
  • Securing the university's needs of faculty members and their assistants.
  • Providing qualification opportunities for those wishing to enroll in postgraduate programs.
  • Directing letters and theses projects to contribute to solving community problems.
  • Preparing qualified cadres in the field of knowledge and research in various disciplines.
  • Contribute to achieving the university's vision, mission and goals.
Our Mission
Seeking to submit postgraduate studies and complete individual and group research projects to contribute to achieving human development and solving local and regional societal problems; Through the efficient use of the university's physical and organizational resources, academic and administrative capabilities and skills, expansion, partnership and cooperation with universities and research institutions.
Dean's Word
l;
Dr.. Muhammad Saeed Al-Haidari - Dean of Graduate Studies and Scientific Research:

It is known that developed countries pay special attention to scientific research, and set high budgets for it, because scientific research is the compass of progress and advancement, and a cornerstone in the course of development and growth through specialized institutions and centers that provide research and advice necessary to formulate strategic policies for countries.

Universities are at the forefront of these institutions that contribute to scientific research through their specialized centers and the programs they offer in the field of graduate studies.

The Department of Graduate Studies and Scientific Research at the University of Science and Technology contributes to the development of scientific research through scientific theses and targeted theses supervised by the department or through the publication of distinguished research presented by faculty members. Providing the community with distinguished and qualified competencies according to modern scientific plans, which we hope will contribute to building and developing the community.

In conclusion, we extend our sincere thanks and appreciation to the leadership of the university for the financial and moral support that it provides to meet the special needs of graduate studies and scientific research.
