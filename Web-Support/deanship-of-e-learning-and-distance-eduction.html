Our Mission
Seeking Deanship of E-Learning and Distance Education The University of Science and Technology aims to provide a distinguished educational service for learners who were unable to enroll in traditional education, to meet the needs of the labor market locally and regionally through highly qualified educational, administrative and technical staff, and to apply the latest educational technologies in accordance with international standards for open education and comprehensive quality, committed to professional ethics. .

Objectives
  • Providing university education opportunities for those who were unable to enroll in traditional education.
  • Providing an educational environment that meets the requirements of e-learning and distance education.
  • Developing and qualifying human cadres to meet the requirements of e-learning and distance education.
  • Contribute to meeting the needs of society and the labor market of qualified cadres.
  • Expanding partnerships with electronic and open universities regionally and internationally.
Admission And Registration Requirements For Deanship of E-Learning and Distance Education.
  • Obtaining a high school diploma in the Republic of Yemen or its equivalent at the rate determined annually.
  • Register through the online portal of the Deanship at the link ust.edu and fill out the online application
  • Send or submit the originals of all documents submitted electronically, certified and certified by the relevant authorities at the university, and sent to the Admission and Registration Department of the Deanship of E-Learning and Distance Education.
  • Admission is made according to the capacity specified by the university and approved by the Ministry of Higher Education.
  • Undertaking to abide by the university's regulations, instructions and regulations in force during the study.
University of Science and Technology Yemen has Over 25 Years of Experience in Providing Distance Learning Globally
Thinking of furthering your studies without the need to be physically present on campus or at a university?

The Deanship of Electronic and Distance Learning at the University of Science and Technology, Yemen allows you to experience quality distance learning education by providing access to the best online learning courses for various programs Through its online portal: smart.ust.edu.
Advantages of Distance Education through E-Learning at UST, Yemen
With over 25 years of experience in distance learning education, UST is considered one of the best distance learning universities in Yemen and provides international standards of open learning for all. Students can have access to their distance learning courses and learn from anywhere in the world to obtain an internationally recognised bachelor's degree. Besides that, UST is also home to quality educators and administrative staff that are committed to providing the best e-learning program in Yemen to distance learning students.
