Student Housing
University housing equipped with the latest facilities and amenities, offering a variety of recreational activities for students, making their days unforgettable memories.
  • Long and short trips and excursions
  • Individual and team sports leagues
  • Cultural weeks, public meetings, and sessions
  • Reception and farewell parties for students
Student Accommodation Services
  • Internet service
  • Cleanliness of all housing facilities except the student’s room, and the necessary sanitary tools and other things
  • Continuous maintenance of housing facilities (carpentry - plumbing - electricity)
  • A gym in each residence with table tennis, billiards, boules and some athletics.
  • Communication service with students' parents.
Female Student Housing
University housing equipped with the latest facilities and amenities, offering a variety of recreational activities for students, making their days unforgettable memories.
  • Short trips and overnight excursions + long trips to nearby provinces.
  • Holding celebrations: entertainment, farewell, etc.
  • Visit various exhibitions.
  • Cultural and scientific competitions.
  • Lectures
  • Flyers, flyers and wall magazines
  • Visiting various charitable organizations.
  • Farewell and honoring parties for graduates.
  • Student guidance for female students
Student Housing Services
  • Service facilities: prayer room - activity hall - sports games - grocery store - visit hall.
  • Rooms equipped with the latest equipment and amenities. Three types of rooms are available (single - double - triple),
  • Continuous maintenance of housing facilities (carpentry - plumbing - electricity)
  • Internet service
  • 24/7 security guard
Procedures for booking a room in a male or female student residence
  • The new student in the housing fills out the housing contract and signs it.
  • Pay the full year's fees or at least six months' fees.
  • Bring a 4*6 personal photo.
  • A copy of the ID card if he is Yemeni.
  • A copy of the passport if he is not Yemeni.
  • If the old student in the residence wishes to renew the reservation for the new year, he must fill out the residence reservation form.
  • Upon his return from travel, he pays the fees he owes.
  • Payment of fees is made through the college accountants.
Other information For male and female students
  • If the student wants to withdraw from the accommodation, he will provide an alternative, provided that it meets the accommodation requirements.
  • If the substitute student is unable to do so, he is obligated to pay the room rent until the end of the year.
  • The student will get back the remaining housing fees if he brings an alternative.
  • Reservations for student housing begin at the end of the academic year for old students, and when admission and registration at the university opens for new students.
  • The housing contract starts on 9/1 and ends on 8/31 (i.e. a full year).
  • The student is allowed to bring a computer or mobile phone into his room, but is prohibited from bringing a television.
  • Visitors are allowed until 10 pm and non-residents are not allowed to stay overnight in the residence without permission.
  • The residence gate closes at 11pm, except for those who have an emergency, in which case permission must be sought from the residence supervisor.
  • Current housing is in the apartment system. The apartment contains three to four rooms, two to three bathrooms, and a shared kitchen for the apartment.
  • Available seats in the residences (single rooms - double rooms - triple rooms).
  • The fees include (rent - electricity and water - maintenance - internet - and a security deposit of 5,000 Yemeni riyals, which will be returned to the student at the end of the year if the furniture provided to him is in good condition).
